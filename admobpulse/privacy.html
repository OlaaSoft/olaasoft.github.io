Privacy Policy

App Name: AdMob Pulse
Developer / Company: OlaaSoft
Effective Date: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how OlaaSoft handles your information when you use the mobile application AdMob Pulse. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers. AdMob Pulse is a viewer for your own Google AdMob account — all earnings and statistics you see in the App are fetched directly from Google's AdMob API to your device.

  • App preferences (theme, currency, selected account) are stored locally on your device only.
  • AdMob API responses (earnings, impressions, eCPM) are cached locally on your device for offline reading and to reduce repeat API calls.
  • We do not store your AdMob data, earnings, or account information on our servers.

2. Google Sign-In and AdMob API Access

To show your AdMob earnings, AdMob Pulse uses Google Sign-In and requests read-only access to your AdMob account through the Google AdMob API:

  • Scope requested: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/admob.readonly — view AdMob reports and configuration only. The App cannot create, modify, or delete anything in your AdMob account.
  • Your Google account email is used only on your device to identify which AdMob account you are viewing. It is never sent to our servers.
  • OAuth tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain / Android Keystore on your device. You can revoke access at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

AdMob Pulse offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (weekly, yearly) and a one-time lifetime purchase. All transactions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not collect or store any payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob API (Google LLC): Used to fetch your AdMob earnings and statistics on your behalf. Read-only access. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics (Google LLC): Used to track crashes and aggregate, anonymous usage patterns. No personal data, AdMob data, or account information is transmitted.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google LLC): Used to deliver optional daily earnings summary push notifications.
  • Mixpanel: Used for anonymized product analytics to improve the app experience. No AdMob data is sent.
  • Sentry: Used for error tracking and crash reporting.
  • Adapty: Used for in-app subscription management only.

5. Notifications

If you opt in, AdMob Pulse may send daily local notifications summarizing your latest earnings. Notifications are scheduled and rendered locally on your device — no notification content is sent through our servers.

6. Offline Use

Cached earnings and statistics remain readable without an internet connection. An internet connection is required to refresh data from the AdMob API and to make in-app subscription purchases.

7. Children's Privacy

AdMob Pulse is a developer tool intended for app publishers and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

8. Data Deletion

Because we do not store your data on our servers, there is nothing to delete on our side. To remove all data on your device, simply uninstall the App. To revoke Google AdMob access, visit myaccount.google.com/permissions.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and/or on this page with a new effective date.
