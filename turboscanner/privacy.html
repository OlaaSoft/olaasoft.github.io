Privacy Policy

TurboScanner by OlaaSoft · Effective: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

OlaaSoft ("we", "our", "us") built TurboScanner as a document scanning application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use TurboScanner.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit your scanned documents. All documents you scan are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

We may collect the following non-personal information to improve the app experience:

  • Analytics data: App usage patterns, screen views, feature usage, and crash reports via Mixpanel and Firebase Analytics. This data is anonymized and cannot identify you personally.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, app version, and language setting.
  • Subscription status: Whether you have an active premium subscription, managed through Adapty.

3. How We Use Your Information

The anonymized analytics data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users
  • Enhance the overall user experience

4. Third-Party Services

TurboScanner uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Analytics and crash reporting — Privacy Policy
  • Mixpanel: Product analytics — Privacy Policy
  • Adapty: Subscription management — Privacy Policy
  • Sentry: Error tracking — Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage & Security

Your scanned documents remain on your device at all times. We do not have access to your documents, images, signatures, or any content you create within the app.

Analytics data is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL).

6. Children's Privacy

TurboScanner is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all your scanned documents at any time from within the app
  • Opt out of analytics by disabling analytics in your device settings
  • Request deletion of any analytics data associated with your device by contacting us

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the effective date.
