Privacy Policy

App Name: TurboScanner
Developer / Company: OlaaSoft
Effective Date: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how OlaaSoft handles your information when you use the mobile application TurboScanner. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers. All user data (such as your scanned documents, images, signatures, and PDF files) are stored locally on your device only.

  • Scanned documents and images
  • Cropped and enhanced pages
  • Digital signatures you create or import
  • Exported PDF files
  • OCR extracted text

We do not store or process any of this data on our servers. All scanning, cropping, and OCR processing is done locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google LLC): Used to understand usage patterns and improve app performance. No personal data (such as names, emails, or user-generated content) is transmitted.
  • Mixpanel: Used for anonymized product analytics to improve the app experience.
  • Adapty: Used for subscription management only.
  • Sentry: Used for error tracking and crash reporting.

3. Offline Use

All core features (scanning, cropping, enhancing, and organizing documents) work without an internet connection. Your data remains stored locally on your device at all times.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 4. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.
